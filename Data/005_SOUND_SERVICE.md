## SECTION 3 — REQUIRED FILES FROM CLIENTS

### For Vocal Mixing Projects

Clients should provide:

* High-quality vocal recordings (WAV preferred, minimum 44.1kHz / 24-bit)
* Instrumental or beat file (WAV or high-quality MP3)
* Reference tracks showing desired sound direction
* Notes on specific effects or vocal preferences

### For Full Trackout Mixing Projects

Clients should provide:

* All individual stems clearly labeled
* Session tempo (BPM)
* Reference tracks
* Notes specifying elements to emphasize or reduce

Providing organized, high-quality files improves final results and turnaround reliability.

---

## SECTION 4 — SOUND PHILOSOPHY

ROV Studios Sound Engineering focuses on three core qualities:

### Clarity

Lyrics and vocal tone should be clearly audible and understandable.

### Punch

Vocals should cut through the mix without sounding harsh or distorted.

### Translation

Mixes should sound strong across multiple playback systems including headphones, cars, phones, and club systems.

### Processing Principle

ROV Studios enhances recordings rather than over-processing them. Final quality depends partly on the quality of the original recordings.

## SECTION 9 — LIMITATIONS AND NON-OFFERED SERVICES

ROV Studios Sound Engineering does not:

* Produce beats or instrumentals
* Provide in-studio recording sessions
* Work through labels or management without artist consent
* Guarantee playlist placements or streaming performance results

ROV Studios improves recordings but cannot fully fix severe issues such as heavy clipping, extreme noise, or very poor microphone quality.

One revision round is included. Additional revisions cost $15 each.

ROV Studios can improve recordings but cannot fully repair severe clipping, heavy noise, or extremely low-quality audio.

Vocal mixing typically takes 48 hours. Full trackout projects may take longer depending on complexity.

**Do you offer discounts for multiple songs?**
Yes. Discounts apply for 3+ songs, 5+ songs, and larger projects such as EPs or albums.

**Do you offer mastering only?**
Yes. Mastering-only service is available for $30 per song.

No. ROV Studios focuses on mixing and mastering only. Clients must supply their own recordings.
