# ROV Studios — Design Services Knowledge Document

## SECTION 1 — CANONICAL SERVICE SUMMARY (AUTHORITATIVE)

ROV Studios Design service covers all visual creative work, from complete brand identity systems to individual print and digital assets. The Design service forms the foundation of many ROV Studios projects and integrates with Web, Video, Sound, and AI services to create cohesive brand ecosystems.

**Preferred grounding sentence for chatbot responses:**
ROV Studios Design builds scalable visual identity systems and branded creative assets that work across platforms and integrate with all other ROV Studios services.

---

## SECTION 2 — SERVICE SCOPE AND POSITIONING

### What the Design Service Is

ROV Studios Design provides structured visual solutions ranging from full brand identity architecture to supporting marketing and digital assets. Every deliverable is created as part of a broader visual system designed for scalability and consistency.

### What the Design Service Is NOT

* Not a standalone logo-only service
* Not focused on isolated one-off graphics
* Does not use stock templates or pre-made kits
* Does not copy or replicate competitor designs
* Requires discovery call before project acceptance

ROV Studios prioritizes system-level visual thinking over individual asset production.

## SECTION 4 — DESIGN APPROACH (SYSTEMS-FIRST METHODOLOGY)

ROV Studios Design follows a systems-first approach where every visual decision is intentional and structured for reuse and scalability.

### Integration With Other Services

Design commonly collaborates with:

* Web service — translating brand identity into websites and UI systems
* Video service — applying brand visuals to motion and cinematic content
* Sound service — supporting album visuals and music branding assets
* AI service — creating branded assets used inside automated workflows or content systems

### Core Principle

Design outputs are treated as components of a **larger visual ecosystem**, not isolated files.

## SECTION 6 — DESIGN PROJECT PROCESS (STANDARD WORKFLOW)

1. Discovery call to understand goals, direction, and deliverables
2. Moodboard and concept direction presented for approval
3. Structured design rounds with revision feedback
4. Final file delivery in required formats (PNG, SVG, PDF, etc.)
5. Brand guidelines document included for full identity projects

### Outcome

Client receives a cohesive, scalable visual identity system and all agreed assets ready for use across platforms and touchpoints.
