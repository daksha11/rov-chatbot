## SECTION 3 — WHAT A DISCOVERY CALL IS

A discovery call is a **30-minute conversation** conducted via Zoom or phone.

### Purpose of the Call

During the call, ROV Studios will:

1. Learn about the client’s project and context
2. Understand goals and success metrics
3. Discuss timeline requirements and urgency
4. Talk about budget expectations
5. Determine whether the project is a good mutual fit

The discovery call ensures proposals are accurate and aligned with real project needs.

---

## SECTION 4 — WHAT CLIENTS SHOULD PREPARE BEFORE BOOKING

To make the discovery call effective, clients should prepare:

* A short description of the project
* Reference examples (websites, songs, videos, designs, etc.)
* Desired timeline or deadline
* Approximate budget range (rough estimate is acceptable)
* Any special requirements or constraints

Preparation improves proposal accuracy and speeds up project kickoff.

* Understanding the process or workflow

* Discussing a specific project
* Starting a real engagement
* Multi-service or complex projects
* Situations requiring detailed back-and-forth discussion

If a user requests anything project-specific, the correct next step is to book a discovery call.

## SECTION 6 — WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THE CALL

### If the Project Is a Good Fit

**Within 24–48 hours**, the client receives a custom proposal including:

* Detailed scope of work
* Timeline breakdown
* Pricing (fixed or quote-based depending on service)
* Payment terms
* Clear next steps

### After Proposal Approval

1. Contract and invoice are sent
2. Client signs agreement
3. Project kickoff begins
