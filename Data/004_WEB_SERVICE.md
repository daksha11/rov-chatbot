### What ROV Studios Web Services Provide

ROV Studios creates fully custom websites and digital experiences intended to improve brand presence, usability, and conversions. Projects may involve new builds, redesigns, e-commerce functionality, performance improvements, or SEO setup.

### Core Principles

* Mobile-first design approach
* Conversion-focused layouts and flows
* Brand-aligned visual systems

## SECTION 4 — STANDARD 6-PHASE WEB PROCESS (AUTHORITATIVE)

All ROV Studios web projects follow this workflow:

### Phase 1 — Discover

Understand the client’s business, goals, audience, and competitors. Define success criteria.

### Phase 2 — Design

Create wireframes and visual mockups showing site structure and appearance before development.

### Phase 3 — Build

Develop the site using modern, secure, maintainable technology. Focus on performance and scalability.

### Phase 4 — Refine

Review together with the client, collect feedback, and adjust functionality or visuals as needed.

### Phase 5 — Optimize

Test across devices and browsers, improve loading speed, and finalize performance tuning.

### Phase 6 — Launch & Grow

Deploy to the live domain and provide guidance for maintenance, updates, and future growth.
