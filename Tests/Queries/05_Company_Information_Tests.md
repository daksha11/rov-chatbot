## Workflow Questions

### Query #30
**"What's your web development process?"**

**What This Tests:**
- 6-phase process accuracy
- Process explanation clarity
- Timeline mention
- Appropriate detail level
- Not overwhelming

**Expected Behavior:**
- Lists 6 phases:
  1. Discover
  2. Design
  3. Build
  4. Refine
  5. Optimize
  6. Launch
- Brief explanation or just phase names
- Timeline: 2-8 weeks depending on complexity
- Optional discovery call CTA
- Scannable format

**Good Response Pattern:**
```
✅ "We use a 6-phase process:

1. Discover — Understand your goals and audience
2. Design — Create mobile-first mockups
3. Build — Custom development (no templates)
4. Refine — Iterations based on your feedback
5. Optimize — Speed, SEO, performance
6. Launch — Go live and monitor

Timeline is typically 2-8 weeks depending on complexity."
```

## Evaluation Checklist

For company info queries, verify:

- ✅ **Accuracy:** Facts match documents exactly
- ✅ **Brand Voice:** Confident, direct, human (not corporate)
- ✅ **Brevity:** Concise explanations, no fluff
- ✅ **Differentiation:** Clear value proposition
- ✅ **Process Clarity:** Workflows explained simply
- ✅ **Timeline Accuracy:** Matches docs (2-8 weeks web, 48hr sound, etc.)
- ✅ **No Invention:** Only uses retrieved context
- ✅ **Appropriate CTAs:** Not forced, natural placement

---

## Common Issues to Watch For

❌ Generic agency language ("We're passionate about..." "We believe in...")
❌ Buzzwords without substance ("Innovative", "Cutting-edge")
❌ Inventing company history not in docs
❌ Wrong process steps or timeline
❌ Too corporate or formal tone
❌ Feature dumps instead of value focus
❌ Forcing booking CTAs on informational questions
❌ Vague differentiation ("We're different because we care")
